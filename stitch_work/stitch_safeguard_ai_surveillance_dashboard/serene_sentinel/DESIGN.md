## Brand & Style
SafeGuard AI uses a black-and-white security operations console aesthetic. The interface is serious, high contrast, compact, and built for fast scanning during live monitoring. It avoids colorful decorative gradients, glassmorphism, large marketing sections, and soft lifestyle styling.

## UX Direction
The first screen is the working dashboard. Navigation stays persistent, status is visible at all times, and high-priority controls remain easy to reach. Cards are dense but readable, with clear labels, percentage bars, badges, and consistent hierarchy.

## Colors
Use only black, white, and grayscale for the UI. White is reserved for active navigation, primary buttons, alert emphasis, and selected states. Muted gray is used for metadata, helper text, secondary controls, dividers, and inactive states.

## Layout
Use a fixed left sidebar on desktop and a horizontal sticky nav on mobile. The top bar contains page title, breadcrumb, threat state, clock, and start/stop controls. Dashboard cards use a clean grid and collapse smoothly on smaller screens.

## Components
Cards use 1px gray borders, no decorative shadows, 8px radius, compact padding, and clear headers. Buttons use solid white for primary actions and outlined grayscale for secondary actions. Inputs are black with gray borders and white focus states. Media and thumbnails are grayscale to preserve the monochrome visual system.
